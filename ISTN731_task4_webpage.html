Introduction to Negative Applications of Global IT Trends

The rapid evolution of Information Technology (IT) has ushered in a new era of innovation and interconnectedness. While these advancements have brought numerous benefits, they also present potential risks and challenges. This essay will delve into three of the key global IT trends identified in the previous activity, examining their negative applications from both a global and local (South African) perspective. By exploring the potential misuse of these technologies, we can gain a better understanding of the threats they pose and develop strategies to mitigate their harmful effects. This analysis will draw upon insights from various sources, including online resources, personal research, and discussions within the learning forum.
